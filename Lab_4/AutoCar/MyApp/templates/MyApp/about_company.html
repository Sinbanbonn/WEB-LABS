History of Establishment

Year 1995: "AutoCar" company was founded in 1995 by Vladimir Ivanov. He became the founder and the first chairman of the company. The idea of creating "AutoCar" came to Vladimir when he faced the problem of finding parking spaces in the busiest parts of the city.

During the first few years, the "AutoCar" company was a small local business that provided services for providing safe and convenient parking spaces for cars. Vladimir used his personal savings and borrowed funds to purchase land plots and create parking spaces.

The company developed a unique system for managing and controlling access to parking lots. It allows car owners to reserve parking spaces in advance and effectively track the entry and exit of cars using electronic key fobs.

Abbreviations

Definitions

  1. Parking Lot - a specialized area for temporary parking of cars.
  2. Parking Space - a designated space for parking a vehicle.
  3. Reservation - the process of pre-ordering and reserving a parking space.
  4. Rental - the service of providing a parking space for a specified period of time.
